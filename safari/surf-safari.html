Surf Safari

Learn to surf on our very own lakes!

We go where the wind blows—literally. Let us take care of everything, with two ISA certified surf instructors we will have you riding waves safely in no time. We check the conditions daily, and send you a weeks warning followed by a confirmation the day before the lesson. We will either set a downtown meeting location for a pickup, or you can drive yourself and meet us at the spot.

Lessons happen on lakes Ontario, Erie, and Huron; the whole experience runs from 7:00am to 7:00pm.

The day includes:

  • Transportation to and from the surf spot
  • Surfboard and wetsuit
  • Lunch and beach refreshments
  • Dry land lesson covering safety, techniques, and surf conditions.
  • In water lesson expanding on your knowledge from the dry land lesson.
  • An afternoon of free surfing with the group and instructors
  • GoPro pictures and videos

*No experience required. Groups are limited to 6 participants maximum.
