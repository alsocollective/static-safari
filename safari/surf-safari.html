Surf Safari

Learn to surf on our very own lakes!

We go where the wind blows (literally). Let us take care of everything, with two ISA certified surf instructors we will have you riding waves in no time. We check the conditions daily and if you have registered for a lesson you get a weeks warning and a confirmation the day before once we know the weather will hold. We will either meet in a central area downtown for a pickup or you can drive yourself to the spot we will share the day before.

The day includes:

  • Transportation to and from the surf spot
  • Surfboard and wetsuit
  • Lunch and beach refreshments
  • Dry land surf lessons where we teach you the mechanics to getting up and riding the wave a long with ocean and lake knowledge
  • In water lessons where we swim near you to get you moving
  • An afternoon of free surfing all together so we can all learn from each other in the water
  • GoPro pictures and videos

*No experience required.
